Solve the following indefinite integral:

\int 2x \, dx = ?

Your Answer

x^2 + C

Correct Solution

x^2 + C

Explanation

Using the power rule of integration, where \int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C. For n=1, the integral becomes 2 \cdot \frac{x^2}{2} + C = x^2 + C.

Calculate the volume of a sphere with radius r:

V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3

Your Answer

\pi r^2

Correct Solution

\frac{4}{3} \pi r^3

Explanation

The formula \pi r^2 represents the area of a circle. The volume of a sphere is derived through spherical integration as V = \int_{0}^{\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} \int_{0}^{r} \rho^2 \sin \phi \, d\rho \, d\theta \, d\phi.

Evaluate the following trigonometric limit:

\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = ?

Your Answer

1

Correct Solution

1

Explanation

Using L'Hôpital's Rule or the Squeeze Theorem, we find that as x approaches 0, the ratio of the arc length to its sine approaches equality.
